GreenUS

Green for you and the planet!

GreenUS is part carbon footprint tool and part social media and news platform. It combines:

  • tracking of one's green behaivor
  • mechanisms to reward such behavior (such as with tree planting)
  • social media functionality to connect green leaders with their communities and like-minded peers.
Bring the world closer together and back onto a lower-carbon, socially-positive trajectory!
